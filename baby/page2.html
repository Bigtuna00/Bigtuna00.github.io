The Little Buddha
11/4/2010
  
 
Ethan had his 1 month pediatrician visit today. 22" long, so he is only about an inch longer, but he weighed 9lbs. 14oz...he seems to be growing horizontally now! :) Sometimes we rub his belly for good luck.

The Dr. said he was perfect, everything looks great!

A Brief Foray Out of the Dungeon...
10/11/2010
  
 
Crawling out of my baby raising cave for a quick update :)

I've added a bunch more photos so click the Photos link to see the latest (I'm not including them here because the are too many at this point).

Took Mia to the dog park today and actually got a little sun. Angel is not so lucky yet :(

Ethan is doing great. We weighed him today, 7lbs 9.8oz so he is almost back to his birth weight of 7lbs 11 oz (which usually takes 1-2 weeks). We got permission from the doctor today to stop supplementing which is great news (we were supplementing because he was jaundiced in case you missed that news). Things are starting to "normalize" where there aren't so many extra steps and we can just to what we're supposed to do.

We had our baby photo shoot yesterday. Ethan wasn't terribly cooperative. It took around 3-4 hours before we called it quits but we are hoping the photographer got some good shots. Ethan Douglas Fletcher entered the world today at 11:07 AM. He weighed in at 7 lbs 11 oz. Mom and baby are doing great!
