Monday, May 31, 1999

 From Josh                                      

wOOhOO, summer's coming!!!

I've seen The Phantom Menace twice, and I love it twice as much!!! Here's some advice, for those of you who haven't seen it: don't go in expecting more than you did from the first Star Wars movie!

When I went to see it, I went in like the 8 year old kid I was when I saw Empire. In other words, don't watch it like an adult, watch it like a wide-eyed kid. You'll have a great time!!!

For those of you who have seen it and can find nothing better to do than complain about how corny it is, go back and watch the first three!!! For those that claim it's "stiffly" acted, this ain't Schindler's List. It's George Lucas for peet's sake!

Ok, 'nuff ranting. Point is, I like the movie a LOT. The pod racing and light sabre duel alone are enough to make it worth seeing, and there is much more for your enjoyment too. Happy viewing!!!
