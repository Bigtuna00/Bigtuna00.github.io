UPDATE: Thanks to the generosity of our friends and family we have been able to donate $500 to the Lance Armstrong Foundation and $500 to Heifer International and we are on our way to owning our first home!Thank you all so much for your contributions!

This page contains information about Angel and Josh's wedding registry and a little background about our choice of registry.

We have met at a point in our lives where we have plenty of "stuff". We felt strongly that we did not want to go the traditional wedding gift route and that we wanted our solution to give something back.

We decided to do a cash-gift wedding registry with two goals in mind:

  1. Half of all conributions will be donated to charity.
  2. The remainder will be used for a down payment on our first home!

We have each chosen a charity with some personal meaning to us and each of these organizations will get 25% of the total contributions. Here is some information about our charity choices:

For her charity Angel has chosen Heifer International. The mission of Heifer International is to work with communities to end hunger and poverty and to care for the earth. For more information check out their website!

Josh has chosen the Lance Armstrong Foundation for his charity. The Lance Armstrong Foundation is a charity devoted to uniting people to fight cancer. As an avid cyclist, and cancer survivor, the decision to support this charity was a no-brainer :) For more information please visit their website!

We hope that this will be a convenient way for our families and friends to participate in our wedding and, at the same time, bring just a bit more meaning to the gift-giving.

Check the Contribute page for information on how to contribute. Thank you so much for your time!
