The map below highlights the streets you will need to know in order to reach both the hotels and Guglielmo Winery in relation to highway 101. Directions are below the map.

From San Jose International Airport to Hotels:

Take 101 South to the East Dunne exit. Turn left off the exit, cross over the freeway and take your first right onto Condit Road. All three hotels are off Condit Rd.

From the Hotels to Guglielmo Winery:

Go North on Condit Road, turn right on East Main. The winery is on your right and parking is on your left at the Live Oak High School.

From 101 to Guglielmo Winery:

Exit the East Dunne exit and go east. Turn left on Condit road then turn right on E Main Ave. The winery is on your right and parking is on your left at the Live Oak High School.
